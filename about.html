Our Story

Urban Bloom was founded on a simple idea: everyone deserves to have a little nature in their life, no matter how small their space. We started in a tiny city apartment, discovering the joy and tranquility that a single houseplant could bring to a busy urban environment. We realized that plants aren't just decor; they're living companions that clean our air, boost our mood, and connect us back to the natural world.

Our mission is to make plant ownership accessible and enjoyable for everyone. We carefully select the hardiest, most beautiful plants and provide you with all the knowledge you need to help them thrive. From our greenhouse to your home, we're with you every step of the way. Welcome to the Urban Bloom family.
