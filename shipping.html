Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

We are committed to delivering your green friends to you in the best possible condition. All orders are processed within 2 business days. Deliveries typically take 3-7 business days depending on your location within India. We ship from our nursery in New Delhi.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information. We offer FREE shipping on all orders above ₹999. For orders below this amount, a flat shipping fee of ₹99 is applied.

Our Packaging

We have developed specialized, eco-friendly packaging to ensure your plant arrives safely. Each plant is secured in its pot to prevent soil spillage, and the box is designed to protect the leaves and stems from damage during transit.

Return & Replacement Policy

As live plants are perishable, we do not accept returns. However, we offer a 7-day replacement guarantee. If your plant arrives in a damaged condition or does not meet your expectations, please email us a photo at support@urbanbloom.com within 7 days of receiving your order. We will gladly send a replacement plant for you.
