Frequently Asked Questions

What if my plant arrives damaged?

We pack our plants with extreme care, but accidents can happen. If your plant arrives damaged, please send a photo to support@urbanbloom.com within 48 hours of delivery, and we will be happy to send you a replacement free of charge.

How do I care for my new plant?

Each plant on our website has a detailed "Plant Care" tab on its product page. Additionally, you can visit the "Plant Care Tips" section linked in our footer for general guidance on watering, light, and soil for various types of houseplants.

Do you ship all over India?

Yes, we ship to most pincodes across India. During checkout, you can enter your pincode to verify if we deliver to your location. We partner with reliable courier services to ensure your plant reaches you safely.

What is your return policy?

Due to the perishable nature of live plants, we do not accept returns. However, if you have any issues with your order, please contact us, and we will do our best to resolve the problem to your satisfaction. Your happiness is our priority!
